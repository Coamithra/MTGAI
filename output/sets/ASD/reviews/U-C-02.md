**Analysis:**

Glintscale Flyer is a straightforward blue common creature with excellent fundamentals. It's a 1/2 Drake with flying for {1}{U}, which is a conservative, well-established statline for the format (directly comparable to Welkin Tern and similar blue flyers from core sets).

**Templating & Mechanics:** The oracle text is clean and correct. Flying is evergreen and needs no reminder text at common. No custom mechanics are present, so no Salvage/Malfunction/Overclock issues apply.

**Balance:** The power budget is solid. P+T = 3, CMC = 2, leaving 1 point of "free" value, which is well under the typical CMC+3 ceiling for common creatures. A 1/2 flying body for {1}{U} is slightly defensive compared to aggressive flying creatures (like 2/1 flyers), but that defensive angle is intentional and matches blue's color pie role as a tempo-oriented but also defensive color at common. This card would be a reasonable playable in limited without being pushed.

**Design Quality:** The card has a focused, singular purpose: be a defensive flyer with evasion. It's french vanilla, which is appropriate and intentional for a common. The flavor text meaningfully connects to the set's megadungeon theme. No kitchen sink problems.

**Color Pie:** Flying in blue is core to the color pie. No issues.

**Keywords & Nonbos:** Flying is clean and has no interactions with other abilities (since there are none). No negated keywords or collisions.

This card is well-designed and ready for print.
