**Analysis:**

Fist Enforcer is a straightforward French vanilla black common at CMC 2. The card presents a 2/1 creature with menace for {1}{B}, fitting squarely within the P+T=3 budget constraint for a two-drop (CMC 2 + 3 = 5 total). 

Menace is a primary black keyword and thematically appropriate for an enforcer character—it represents an intimidating presence that's difficult to block efficiently. The 2/1 stat line with menace is well-established in black's design space; comparable cards include Bloodghast variants and other aggressive two-drops with evasion effects. The low toughness balances the evasion, making it vulnerable to common removal and combat tricks.

The card has no custom mechanics, so reminder text is not required. The oracle text is clean and uses standard MTG templating. There are no keyword collisions, no negated abilities, no kitchen sink effects, and no conditional logic. Color pie adherence is strong—menace is a core black mechanic, and the aggressive human soldier archetype fits black's raider/soldier themes well, especially in a post-apocalyptic setting.

From a balance perspective, this card is appropriately costed for common. It's a solid, efficient two-drop that will function as a reasonable early threat in limited but isn't so powerful that it warps the format. The design is focused and intentional: a simple, aggressive creature that does exactly what it sets out to do.
