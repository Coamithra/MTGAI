**Analysis:**

Wasteland Raptor is a straightforward red creature designed as a French vanilla aggressive common. The card has clean, unambiguous templating: a 3/1 dinosaur with haste for {2}{R}, which aligns perfectly with red's aggressive philosophy.

**Balance Assessment:** At common, a 3/1 haste creature for 3 mana is appropriately costed. This matches the rate of analogous cards like Embercleave-era red commons. The P/T sum of 4 and CMC+3=6 metric both fall well within typical design parameters. The glass cannon statline (high power, low toughness) is classic red design and provides counterplay through blocking. The card is not above or below rate for its rarity.

**Mechanics & Keywords:** The card uses only haste, a core evergreen keyword with no reminder text needed at common. There are no custom mechanics present. No keyword negations or collisions.

**Design Purpose:** The card has a focused purpose—aggressive early game creature with ambush capability. The flavor text effectively communicates the predator ambush theme ("hunting cry... it was already too late"), and the dinosaur type ties coherently into the set's wilderness faction. No kitchen sink issues.

**Color Pie:** Haste is quintessentially red. The aggressive stats are appropriate for red. No color pie violations.

**Templating:** Oracle text is correctly formatted as a single-line keyword. All required information is present.

This is a well-executed common creature with clear purpose, appropriate power level, and good flavor integration.
