**Mechanics & Templating:**
The card uses two custom mechanics appropriately: the trigger references artifacts entering under your control (no reminder text needed—this is core MTG templating), and the activated ability is cleanly templated with straightforward ramp effect. No custom mechanic keywords (Salvage, Malfunction, Overclock) appear on this card, so no reminder text is required.

**Balance:**
At {1}{G}, the card costs 2 mana with 1/2 stats (p/t = 3, which is modest for rare). The first ability directly synergizes with the set's artifact theme (Salvage cards) by converting artifacts into +1/+1 counters. This is reasonable—it's card advantage in the form of creature growth, but requires an outside engine (Salvage or other artifact generators) to be effective. The activated ability {G}, remove 2 counters: fetch a tapped basic land is strong but comes with a real cost (two counter removal per activation). Tapping the land prevents this from enabling explosive mana sequences. This is roughly comparable to effects like Elvish Archdruid's ramp modes—good in a deck built around artifacts, but not bonkers at 2 CMC. The delayed ramp (requiring counter accumulation first) and tapped land both provide appropriate friction.

**Design Quality:**
The card has a single, focused purpose: turn artifact triggers into sustainable growth and late-game ramp. There are no unrelated effects (not a kitchen sink design). The mechanical coherence is excellent: Salvage cards find artifacts → artifacts trigger growth → accumulated counters fuel ramp. This creates meaningful deckbuilding tension (you need artifact generators to justify the card) and gameplay depth.

**Color Pie:**
Green's signature effect is ramp, and this card delivers that. Green also has artifact synergy support in recent sets (e.g., Tezzeret, master of the Bridge effects, artifacts matter themes in Kaladesh blocks). The counter-growing aspect is green-aligned (green grows creatures). The artifact synergy is a modern color-pie bridge (green can have artifact synergy, especially in artifact sets). Checks out well.

**Keyword Interactions:**
No keywords on this card are negated or made useless by other abilities.

**Reminder Text:**
No custom mechanics appear on this card, so reminder text is not applicable.

**Conditional Checks:**
No conditionals appear that need evaluation.

**Variability:**
The counter accumulation and ramp output are both meaningful—they scale based on actual artifact triggers and deck composition, not fixed values.

**Potential Edge Cases:**
- The artifact trigger is clean and will work as intended with Salvage and other artifact ETB effects.
- No enters-tapped irrelevance.
- No keyword collisions.

This is a tight, well-balanced card with clear mechanical role and excellent design coherence.
