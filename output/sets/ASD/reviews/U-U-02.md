**Analysis:**

Relay Node Technician is a well-designed uncommon that fits cleanly into the Anomalous Descent set's themes. The 2/3 body for {2}{U} is on-rate for uncommon (P+T = 5, mana value 3 puts it in the fair zone—comparable to cards like Electrify or Cerulean Drake). 

The ETB ability is elegant and focused: "When ~ enters, you may remove a counter from target permanent you control. If you do, draw a card." This creates meaningful play patterns without overstepping:

1. **Synergy, not forced consistency:** The condition is genuinely optional and meaningful. You can only draw if you have a counterable permanent, and you need to choose to remove a counter. This isn't a nonbo—it's a payoff for deck building (pairing with Malfunction artifacts or +1/+1 counter sources).

2. **Color pie:** Blue absolutely gets card draw contingent on artifacts or counter-manipulation effects (see cards like Thoughtcast, Galvanic Relay, or Whirza-style constructs). The ability to interact with Malfunction artifacts is firmly within blue's artifact-friendly color pie.

3. **Archetype support:** The card directly supports the UR (Cult of Science/Mad Science) counter-manipulation theme and WU (Ancient Technology) artifact synergies by making Malfunction a genuine value play rather than pure tempo loss. This is good design—it gives payoffs to the set's mechanics.

4. **Balance:** As a 2-for-1 when it works (body + draw), it's not overpowered. Compare to Mulldrifter (a 2/2 flying for {1}{U}{U} that draws 2 cards) or even Growth Spiraling (which does similar counter removal + card draw work). The fact that Relay Node requires a target and an action makes it more restrictive, keeping it appropriately costed for uncommon.

5. **Templating:** Oracle text is clean and modern. The "if you do" clause is correct MTG templating for conditional card draw tied to an action.

6. **No custom mechanics used:** The card does not reference Salvage, Malfunction, or Overclock directly, so there are no reminder text issues to flag.

This is a solid uncommon with good synergy potential and fair power level.
