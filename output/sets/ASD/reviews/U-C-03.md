Redirect Pulse is a blue common instant that performs bounce + cantrip, a classic and well-established blue temporary removal strategy. The card is straightforward and well-templated.

**Templating**: Oracle text follows modern MTG conventions correctly. The card uses proper punctuation and structure for a two-part effect (return target creature, then draw a card). No templating issues.

**Balance**: At {2}{U} (3 mana), this card is appropriately costed. Comparable cards include Repulse ({1}{U}{W}, 3 mana) which does the same thing with slightly different color identity, and Blink of an Eye ({U}, 1 mana) without kicker. Redirect Pulse is straightforward blue bounce without additional modal complexity. The cantrip element (draw a card) is the compensating effect that makes this rate acceptable. The card doesn't break the rate for blue removal at common—it's on par with established precedents. Not above rate, not below rate.

**Design Quality**: The card has a focused, singular purpose: tempo-efficient removal via bounce with card advantage. The flavor text appropriately ties the mechanical effect to the set's post-apocalyptic megadungeon setting (automated defense systems). This is a solid, clean design with no kitchen sink elements.
