## Color pie quick-reference

You know the color pie. As a sanity check, the primary homes of each effect:

- W: creature-removal (exile), tokens, lifegain, pump/anthems, evasion, enchantments
- U: card draw, counterspells, bounce, mill, evasion, artifact synergy
- B: creature-removal (kill), discard, graveyard recursion, sacrifice, life-as-cost
- R: direct damage, haste, temporary pump, artifact destruction, impulsive draw
- G: ramp, big creatures, +1/+1 counters, fight, enchantment/artifact destruction, reach

A tertiary-color placement is fine; an effect wholly outside its colors' pies (counterspell in R, direct damage in U) is not.

## Design constraints

1. Reminder text MUST be under 100 characters.
2. Each mechanic should appear in 2-3 colors (not all 5, not just 1).
3. At least one mechanic must be complexity 1 (viable at common with simple reminder text).
4. Mechanics must support Limited play in a {set_size}-card set.
5. Each mechanic needs clear design space for common, uncommon, and rare versions.
6. Mechanics should reinforce at least 2 draft archetypes from the list above.
7. Avoid parasitic mechanics — they should work with normal MTG cards too.
8. For a {set_size}-card set, plan each mechanic for roughly {expected_mechanic_density} cards.
