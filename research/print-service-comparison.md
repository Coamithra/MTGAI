# Print Service Comparison for Custom MTG Set

## 1. Executive Summary

**Recommendation: MakePlayingCards (MPC)** is the clear winner for printing a custom MTG set of ~280 cards, shipping to the Netherlands. MPC dominates the custom MTG proxy community (r/mpcproxies), offers the closest card stock to real MTG cards (S33), supports unique images per card at scale (up to 612 per deck), and claims to handle EU import tax/customs for standard shipping. No EU-based printer offers comparable per-card pricing at low quantities with per-card customization.

## 4. Shipping and Customs Considerations for NL Delivery

### 4.1 VAT (BTW) Rules
- Netherlands VAT rate: 21%
- EU Import One-Stop Shop (IOSS): Sellers registered for IOSS collect VAT at point of sale for shipments under EUR 150, so buyer pays no additional VAT on delivery
- For shipments over EUR 150 without IOSS: buyer pays 21% VAT + potential customs duties on delivery

### 4.2 MPC's EU Shipping Policy
MPC's FAQ explicitly states: "There are no import tax and customs duty issues for shipments to EU countries shipped via standard shipping method." This strongly suggests MPC is registered for IOSS or uses a fulfillment method that pre-clears customs for EU standard shipments. This is a major cost advantage.

**Important:** Use standard shipping (not express) to benefit from this policy. Express shipping may not include the same customs handling.

### 4.3 Risk Mitigation
- Order a small test batch (20-36 cards) first to verify:
  - No unexpected customs charges on delivery
  - Print quality meets expectations
  - Color accuracy is acceptable
  - Card stock feel is satisfactory
- Keep order value under EUR 150 for the test batch to minimize customs risk

## 6. Technical Integration Notes

### File Naming for MPC Upload
Cards should be named for easy sorting during upload:
```
{set_code}_{collector_number:03d}_{card_name_slug}.png
```
Example: `CUS_001_lightning_bolt.png`

### Render Pipeline Output
The card renderer should produce files at exactly:
- **822 x 1122 pixels** at **300 DPI**
- **sRGB** color space
- **PNG** format
- All critical content (text, symbols, card name) within the **750 x 1050 safe area** (36px inset from each edge)
- Card art and frame extend to the full 822x1122 canvas to provide bleed

### Card Back Strategy
Options for card backs:
1. **Single custom back** — Design one back, apply to all cards (simplest)
2. **MTG-style back** — Use a custom design that evokes MTG without copying (avoid trademark issues)
3. **Per-card backs** — Different backs per card (MPC supports this, but adds upload complexity)

Recommendation: Design a single custom set back for all cards. This simplifies the MPC upload process significantly.
